Admission Process to Technical Programs

Government Technical College of Ibadan offers comprehensive admission opportunities for students seeking practical technical and vocational training. Our admission process is designed to identify motivated students who are ready to develop hands-on skills in various technical fields. We welcome applications from qualified candidates who have completed their basic education and are eager to pursue a career in technical trades.

The college provides a supportive learning environment with modern workshops, experienced instructors, and industry-standard equipment. Students gain practical experience through workshop training, ensuring they are job-ready upon graduation. Our programs are designed to meet industry demands and prepare students for successful careers in Nigeria's growing technical sectors.

Application Registration & Requirements:

To apply for admission to Government Technical College of Ibadan, follow these simple steps. Ensure you have all required documents ready before visiting the school's administrative office.

  • 1. Visit the school at Orita Aperin Market, After, Orita Apeni-Bere Road, Agodi gate Ibadan
  • 2. Collect and complete the application form at the administrative office
  • 3. Submit required documents (Birth Certificate, JSS3 Certificate, passport photos)
  • 4. Pay the application fee and await admission notification

What to Expect During Admission

Our admission process evaluates your readiness for technical training. Here's what you need to know to prepare for a successful application to our technical programs.

Meet Entry Requirements

Ensure you have completed basic education (JSS3 or equivalent) and possess the necessary certificates and documents for application.

Choose Your Program

Select from 12 technical programs based on your interests and career goals in engineering, construction, arts, or business.

Visit the Campus

Tour our facilities, meet instructors, and see our workshops to better understand the practical training environment.

Complete Application

Submit all required documents and application fees at the administrative office to finalize your admission process.
