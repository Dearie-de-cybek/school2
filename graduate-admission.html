About Us

Government Technical College of Ibadan, established in 1995, is a premier technical and vocational institution dedicated to equipping students with practical skills and knowledge for the modern workforce. We offer a comprehensive range of technical programs. Our commitment is to develop skilled craftsmen and technicians who contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's technological and economic development through hands-on training and quality education.

How To Apply For Admission

Welcome to Government Technical College of Ibadan! We are excited that you're interested in joining our institution to develop practical technical skills. Our admission process is straightforward and designed to help you begin your journey toward a successful technical career. Follow the steps below to complete your application and secure your place in one of our 12 comprehensive technical and vocational programs.

  • Visit The School
  • Collect Application Form
  • Complete The Form
  • Submit Required Documents
  • Pay Application Fee
  • Await Admission Decision

Required Documents

To complete your admission application, please prepare the following documents: Birth Certificate or Age Declaration, Primary School Leaving Certificate or equivalent, Junior Secondary School Certificate (JSS3), passport photographs, and proof of residence. All documents should be original copies or certified true copies.

Bring all required documents to the school's administrative office located at Orita Aperin Market, After, Orita Apeni-Bere Road, Agodi gate, Ibadan. Our staff will assist you with the application process and answer any questions you may have.

Contact Admissions Office

Tuition & Fees

As a government technical college, we offer affordable technical education to help students gain valuable skills without financial burden. Our tuition fees are subsidized by the government to make quality technical training accessible to all qualified students.

For detailed information about tuition fees, payment schedules, and any available scholarships or support programs, please contact our administrative office at 08055841001 or visit the school in person. Our staff will provide you with complete fee structure information.
