About Us

Government Technical College of Ibadan, established in 1995, is a premier technical and vocational institution dedicated to equipping students with practical skills and knowledge for the modern workforce. We offer a comprehensive range of technical programs. Our commitment is to develop skilled craftsmen and technicians who contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's technological and economic development through hands-on training and quality education.
