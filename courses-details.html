Electrical Installation & Maintenance

  • Department: Engineering

  • Last Update:November 05, 2025

What Will You Learn?

The Electrical Installation & Maintenance program at Government Technical College of Ibadan provides comprehensive training in electrical systems, wiring, installation, and maintenance. Students gain hands-on experience with electrical circuits, safety procedures, and industry-standard equipment. This program prepares you for a successful career as a qualified electrician in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.

Our experienced instructors guide you through practical workshops where you'll learn to install electrical systems, troubleshoot problems, and maintain electrical equipment safely and efficiently. Upon completion, graduates are equipped with nationally recognized skills and ready for employment or entrepreneurship in the electrical trade.

Practical Workshop Training

Gain hands-on experience in our fully equipped electrical workshops. Learn to work with real electrical systems, tools, and equipment under expert supervision to build job-ready skills.

Electrical Safety Standards

Master essential safety procedures and regulations for electrical work. Learn to identify hazards, use protective equipment, and follow industry safety standards to ensure safe work practices.

Troubleshooting & Maintenance

Develop skills in diagnosing electrical faults, repairing systems, and performing preventive maintenance. Learn systematic approaches to problem-solving in electrical installations.

Program Curriculum

Our 3-year Electrical Installation program covers essential theory and extensive practical training. Students progress through foundational concepts to advanced electrical systems, gaining certification in electrical installation and maintenance.

  • Module 1: Introduction to Electrical EngineeringYear 1
  • Module 2: Basic Electrical Circuits & ComponentsYear 1
  • Module 3: Electrical Wiring & Installation TechniquesYear 1
  • Module 4: Electrical Safety & RegulationsYear 2
  • Module 5: Advanced Circuit Design & AnalysisYear 2
  • Module 6: Industrial Electrical SystemsYear 2
  • Module 7: Troubleshooting & MaintenanceYear 3
  • Module 8: Professional Practice & CertificationYear 3
