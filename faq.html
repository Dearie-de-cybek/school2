About Us

Government Technical College of Ibadan, established in 1995, is a premier technical and vocational institution dedicated to equipping students with practical skills and knowledge for the modern workforce. We offer a comprehensive range of technical programs. Our commitment is to develop skilled craftsmen and technicians who contribute meaningfully to Nigeria's technological and economic development through hands-on training and quality education.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about admission, programs, or campus life at Government Technical College of Ibadan? Find answers to the most common questions below.

To gain admission, you must have completed basic education (JSS3 or equivalent) and possess the following: Birth Certificate or Age Declaration, Primary School Leaving Certificate, Junior Secondary School Certificate, passport photographs, and proof of residence. Visit our administrative office at Orita Aperin Market, After, Orita Apeni-Bere Road, Agodi gate Ibadan to submit your application.

We offer 12 comprehensive technical programs: Electrical Installation, Blocklaying Bricklaying and Concreting, Mechanical Engineering Craft, Graphics Art, Painting and Decorating, Catering Craft, Business Studies, Carpentry and Joinery, Furniture Making, Radio and Television Electronics, Motor Vehicle Mechanics, and Basic Education. All programs combine classroom theory with hands-on practical training.

Most of our technical programs run for 3 years, providing comprehensive training in your chosen field. We also offer short courses and specialized certification programs for working professionals who want to upgrade their skills. Contact our administrative office for specific program duration details.

As a government technical college, our tuition fees are subsidized and very affordable to make quality technical education accessible to all students. For detailed information about current tuition fees, payment schedules, and available support programs, please contact our administrative office at 08055841001 or visit the school in person.

Admission typically opens at the beginning of each academic session. We recommend visiting the school or calling 08055841001 to inquire about current admission status and deadlines. Our administrative staff will guide you through the application process and provide all necessary information about upcoming admission periods.
